Biography

Khing (KhingWei Bai) is a Taiwanese artist from Taipei, currently based in Hamburg, Germany.

With a study background in architecture, Khing integrates spatial awareness and movement into his artistic practice, working primarily in photography and installation, which engage viewers in dialogues about identity, sexuality, and the dynamics between artist, artwork, and audience. His work reflects the experience of navigating an outsider’s perspective as (including but not limited to) an Asian in Western contexts, exploring themes of power relations, shifting perspectives, and body image.

His studies at HFBK Hamburg with Annika Larson, Angela Bulloch, and Tobias Zielony have shaped his exploration of audience interaction and identity within his art.
